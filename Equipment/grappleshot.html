Grappleshot

A rapid fire out hook and get quickly reeled in towards the hook point. You can grapple enemies to close in for a kill (which combos really well with the Energy Sword), use it to quickly cross a map with a good swing, or you can pull yourself up to hard-to-reach ledges very quickly. It can even pick up weapons and CTF Flags from afar!

~Maximum number of uses: 5~
